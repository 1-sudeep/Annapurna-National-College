Introduction

Annapurna National College in ............. by a group of professional academicians with a mission to impart practical based quality education to the students to bring change in the behavior, conduct attitude a perspective of looking at the world and people around them. Mission

To achieve and sustain excellene in teaching and research; enriching local, national and international communities through our exploration, professionalism, academic excellence and knowledge advancement.

Vision

To provide demand-based education from play group to Post Graduate level focusing on the all-round development of the students where faculty and studentes shall share, interact and promote each other, locally nationally and internationally, shaping the young minds in terms of academic excellency and intellectual competence.

Values

  • Quality enhancement
  • Sincerity in relationship
  • Adherence to regulations
  • Life-long Learning:
    • encouraging enthusisatic, independent thinkers and learners striving for personal growth.
  • Integrity:
    • Behaving ethically in all interactions at all levels.
  • Partnership:
    • A three cornered partnership among the college, the home and the student.

Objectives

True education should produce balanced personally whose intellectual, emotional and spiritual values are harmoniously developed. Therefore, all the learners in the college are structured to incluate in the learners the qualities of:

  • Self-confidence
  • Academic Excellence
  • Harmonishing positive thoughts, words and actions
  • Instilling the value of selfless service to the society.
  • Maintenance of a well-nourished, healthy and agile physique.
  • Communication and social skills along with analytical abilites.
  • Upholding reverence for parents, elders and dignity of all men.
