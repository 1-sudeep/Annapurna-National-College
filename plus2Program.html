SCIENCE STREAM

Annapurna National, a top +2 science college in Nepal. It has its own glorious history in the realm of education in Nepal especially in science studies. In terms of academic performance it has become the top college in Nepal. Laboratories: State of art laboratories Research activities: students enjoy researching at laboratories to develop their research capacity. And they are succeeded to perform different experiments. Board top results: Pinnacle college students have glorified annapurna college by positioning themselves as FIRST in board exam results many times: We have Top results in final exams every year. Post study achievements: Every single student, as per our knowledge, has been able to get opportunities.Since its establishment till today, every year many students won scholarship to study medical science and engineering in reputed institutions in the home country and abroad.
